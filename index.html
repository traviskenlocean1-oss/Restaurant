Our Story

Born Between
Two Cultures.

KŌSAI — meaning "radiance" in Japanese — was born from a single idea: that the precision of Japanese culinary tradition and the bold, electric energy of Miami are more alike than they appear.

We source fish flown in from Tsukiji twice weekly, pair it with Florida-grown produce, and finish every plate over binchotan charcoal imported from Wakayama. The technique is Japanese. The soul is Miami.

"Every dish is a conversation between two cities."
